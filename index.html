Key Features While Writing A Linked In Bio

  1. Always consider your grammar
  2. If you have to include your hobbies, attach it to the impact it has to your career
  3. Always include a Call To Action
  4. Make sure your statements are not too wordy
  5. In some cases you are allowed to include your skills and achievements
